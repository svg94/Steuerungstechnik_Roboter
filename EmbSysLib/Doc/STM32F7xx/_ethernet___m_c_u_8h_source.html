186  an ethernet packet is received, the Rx-DMA will transfer the packet from RxFIFO
187  to the driver receive buffers memory.
188 
189  Depending on the size of the received ethernet packet and the size of
190  each ethernet driver receive buffer, the received packet can take one or more
191  ethernet driver receive buffer.
192

194  and the total count of the driver receive buffers ETH_RXBUFNB.

197  example, they can be reconfigured in the application layer to fit the application
198  needs */
