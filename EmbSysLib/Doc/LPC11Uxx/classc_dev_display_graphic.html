Detailed Description

Graphic display.

Warning
This is a draft. The string length must not exceed 30 characters

The screen coordinates are zero in the upper left corner.

  • 'x': left to right
  • 'y': top to bottom

Colors are 16 bit RGB. Some colors are predefined in cHwDisplayGraphic::colorPredefined
