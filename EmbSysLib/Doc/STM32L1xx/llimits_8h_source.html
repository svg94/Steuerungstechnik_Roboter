273 ** modulo: defined as 'a - floor(a/b)*b'; this definition gives NaN when
274 ** 'b' is huge, but the result should be 'a'. 'fmod' gives the result of
275 ** 'a - trunc(a/b)*b', and therefore must be corrected when 'trunc(a/b)
